Frequently Asked Questions

What is ChaseBot?

ChaseBot is an AI agent that monitors your outstanding invoices and automatically sends follow-up emails at the right intervals and tone. It escalates overdue invoices and keeps a full paper trail.

Will automated follow-ups damage my client relationships?

No — ChaseBot's tone engine is designed to maintain relationships. Early follow-ups are friendly reminders. Tone escalates gradually only if the client is unresponsive. You can review and customise every template.

What invoicing tools does ChaseBot work with?

ChaseBot integrates with Xero, FreshBooks, Wave, and accepts direct invoice uploads. It is not an invoicing tool itself — it is the collection layer that works alongside what you already use.

How long does setup take?

Setup takes under 5 minutes. Connect your invoicing tool, configure your follow-up schedule, and ChaseBot starts monitoring immediately.
