Frequently Asked Questions

What is ChaseBot?

ChaseBot is an AI agent that monitors your outstanding invoices and automatically sends follow-up emails at the right intervals and tone. It escalates overdue invoices and keeps a full paper trail — without you lifting a finger.

How much revenue do freelancers lose to unpaid invoices?

Studies and freelancer surveys consistently show 15–30% of billable work goes uncollected — primarily because chasing clients is uncomfortable and time-consuming. ChaseBot handles the entire follow-up process so nothing falls through the cracks.

Is ChaseBot invoicing software?

No. ChaseBot is the collection layer — it works alongside your existing invoicing tools (Xero, FreshBooks, Wave, or even a spreadsheet). It monitors what's outstanding and handles the follow-up sequence so you don't have to.

How does ChaseBot decide when and how to follow up?

ChaseBot uses configurable rules and AI tone-matching. It sends a friendly reminder at 3 days overdue, a firmer follow-up at 7 days, and escalates the tone and frequency as the invoice ages — all customisable.
