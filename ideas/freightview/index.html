Frequently Asked Questions

What does FreightView do?

FreightView gives your drivers a mobile app to update delivery status in real time and gives your customers a self-serve tracking portal — so they can see where their shipment is without calling you.

How long does setup take?

Most carriers are live within a day. Drivers download the app, dispatchers set up routes, and your customer portal is ready to share.

How much does it cost?

FreightView starts at /month for up to 10 drivers. No per-delivery fees, no enterprise contracts.

Do my customers need to install anything?

No. Your customers track via a web link — no app download required.
