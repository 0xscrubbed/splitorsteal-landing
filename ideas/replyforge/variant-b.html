Common questions

Why do Reddit posts about my product get removed?

Reddit mods and algorithms flag posts that look promotional. Posts that survive lead with genuine value — answering a real question — and mention the product only when directly relevant.

Is this against Reddit's rules?

Being helpful is not against the rules. Blatant self-promotion is. ReplyForge is built around the helpful-first framework — disclose you built the product when relevant, but the reply has to stand on its own as useful.
