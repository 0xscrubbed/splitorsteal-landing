Common questions

Why do Reddit posts about my product keep getting removed?

Reddit mods and algorithms flag posts that look promotional. The posts that survive are ones that lead with genuine value — answering a real question — and mention the product only when directly relevant.

Is this against Reddit's rules?

Being helpful is not against the rules. Blatant self-promotion is. ReplyForge is built around the helpful-first framework — disclose you built the product when relevant, but the reply has to stand on its own as useful.
