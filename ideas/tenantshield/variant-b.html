Frequently Asked Questions

What is TenantShield?

TenantShield is an app for Australian renters that explains your legal rights in plain English, helps you draft formal notices and dispute letters, and guides you through the VCAT, NCAT, or state tribunal process.

How much do Australian renters lose to unfair bond deductions?

Estimates suggest Australian renters collectively lose hundreds of millions of dollars annually to bond deductions that are unlawful or exaggerated. Most renters accept the deduction because they don't know they can dispute it — or how to.

Which states does TenantShield cover?

TenantShield covers all Australian states and territories — NSW, VIC, QLD, WA, SA, TAS, ACT and NT — with legislation specific to your jurisdiction.

Is TenantShield legal advice?

TenantShield provides legal information and document drafting tools, not formal legal advice. For complex disputes, we recommend consulting a tenancy lawyer or community legal centre.
