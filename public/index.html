NOT CREDIT CARD ROULETTE

Credit card roulette is luck.
This is strategy.

Credit card roulette outsources the outcome to chance. Nobody thinks, nobody decides — you just spin and hope. Split or Steal is different. Every outcome is the direct result of what you chose.

The key difference

Your outcome depends on your read of the room. If you pick steal and everyone else cooperates, you win. If everyone defects, one person gets picked at random — and it might be you. That's not luck. That's game theory.

Unlike credit card roulette, Split or Steal makes everyone accountable. You control your fate — until you don't. The prisoner's dilemma applied to your dinner bill.
